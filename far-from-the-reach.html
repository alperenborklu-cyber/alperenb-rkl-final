Far From The Reach (Only For Today)

Year: 2022 | Role: Director & Editor | Genre: Experimental

Synopsis

A poetic short film focusing on the fleeting nature of moments and memory. The film uses a minimalist visual language to capture the essence of a single day.

Production Notes

"I wanted to capture the beauty in the mundane, focusing on rhythm and atmosphere over traditional plot."

The production focused on high-contrast visual systems and intentional pacing to evoke the core themes of the narrative.
