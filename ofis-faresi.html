Ofis Faresi (Office Mouse)

Year: 2023 | Role: Director & Editor | Genre: Experimental

Synopsis

A psychological exploration of modern workspace environments and the isolation of routine. Ofis Faresi follows a protagonist trapped in a cyclical office life, blending experimental cinematography with surrealist narrative elements.

Production Notes

"This film was born out of a desire to visualize the 'invisible' weight of repetitive modern labor."

The production focused on high-contrast visual systems and intentional pacing to evoke the core themes of the narrative.
